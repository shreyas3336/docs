## Our Vision
To pioneer the AI Agents ecosystem, democratizing AI by empowering creators and users alike. We envision a future where intelligent agents become a novel, liquid asset class, fostering innovation and shared prosperity within a decentralized and transparent framework.

## Our Mission
To deliver an integrated suite – the Capx SuperApp, Capx Chain, and Capx Cloud – that seamlessly facilitates the entire lifecycle of AI agents. We aim to empower creators to build, tokenize, and monetize their AI innovations, while enabling users to discover, invest in, and utilize these agents, all within a robust, community-centric network
