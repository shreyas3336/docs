  <Accordion title="What is the Capx Super App?">
    Capx Super App brings discovery, ownership, and trading of AI-agent apps together in a single, seamless hub. Think of it as the perfect blend of the AI App Store + Robinhood for AI tokens. Users can discover, use, and trade tokenized AI agents, turning them into ownable digital assets. For builders, it offers a new distribution and monetization channel; for users, it provides early access, ownership, and potential upside in the AI agents they use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are AI agents?">
    AI agents are autonomous systems that perceive their environment, process information, make decisions, and take actions on your behalf to achieve specific goals. They can adapt to changes, learn from experience, and operate independently of humans in various tasks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Capx Super App different from a normal app store?">
    The normal App store acts as a great discoverability platform, on the other hand Capx Super App not only helps discover the upcoming and latest AI Agents Apps but also enables users to buy and own the AI Agents they use. Turning users into part owners of tokenized AI Agent Apps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the Capx Super App on mainnet or testnet?">
    Capx Super App that is on Capx Chain, is currently in testnet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need real money to use the Capx Super App during the testnet?">
    No, you don't need real money. During the incentivized testnet, you earn $COINs (the internal point system of Capx Super App, especially introduced as part of Capx incentivized testnet program) by completing quests. These $COINs can be used for trading AI agent tokens on Capx Super App.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I sign up for the Capx Super App?">
    Go to the Capx Super App, and simply sign up using Telegram.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'tradable' agent mean?">
    Tradable AI agents are the tokenized AI Agents, that can be owned and traded on the Capx Super App.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I buy AI agent tokens?">
    As part of the incentivized testnet, all you got to do is earn $COINs and use them to BUY/SELL AI agent tokens on the Capx Super App.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I use $COIN to trade?">
    Go to the "Trade" section, select an AI agent, click "Buy," enter the amount of $COIN you want to spend, and place your trade order.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is slippage in trades, and how do I adjust it?">
    Slippage is the price difference between when a trade is placed and when it's executed. During high network activity, your trade might fail or be delayed. You can increase the slippage percentage to ensure faster processing by allowing for slight token losses. Adjust slippage in the trade window.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I earn Coins on Capx Super App?">
    You can earn Coins by completing quests, inviting your friends to the Capx Super App using your referral code, and by ranking in the top 50 weekly traders.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any daily trading or earning limits?">

  <Accordion title="Where do I find my referral code?">
    Your referral code can be found in both the "Earn" section as well as on your Capx profile page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the referral program work?">
    Capx uses a multi-tier referral system:
    - Parent (you) earns 100 Coins per Child you invite
    - Parent also earns 60 Coins when a Grandchild (your child's referral) joins
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is 'trading volume' on Capx Super App?">
    Trading volume is the total value of your AI agent trades. For example, if you buy for 100 $COIN and sell for 50 $COIN, your volume is 150 $COIN. You can view daily, weekly, and all-time stats.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does my weekly leaderboard rank reset?">
    The weekly leaderboard resets every Monday to create a level playing field and gamify the trading experience. Your all-time volume remains unaffected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the leaderboard and its benefits?">
    The leaderboard shows the top 100 traders each week based on trading volume. The top 50 traders receive 1,000 Coins at the end of the week.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I view my Capx username and wallet address?">
    Click on the profile icon (top-right corner). Your username (e.g., @xyz) and wallet address are displayed there.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is the wallet created during signup?">
    When you sign up via Telegram, a self-custodial wallet is automatically created using Privy and linked to your account. You retain full control though.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my wallet outside of Capx Super App?">
    Yes. In the wallet section, click the gear icon and select "Export Wallet" to access your private key.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which wallet is considered my 'primary' wallet?">
    The wallet created as part of Capx Super App signup is your primary wallet. All your transactions and rewards are linked to this address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I send tokens from one Capx account to another?">
    Yes, tokens can be transferred between Capx accounts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I send AI agent tokens to an external wallet?">
    Yes, you can send AI agent tokens to any external wallet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I claim $GAS for transactions?">
    Click the wallet icon, ensure you're on your primary wallet, and then click the faucet icon to claim GAS tokens for transactions.
  </Accordion>
